.. warning::

   Please have a look to `gforge page <https://gforge.inria.fr/projects/morpheme-privat>`_
   Just contact `Morpheme Team <http://www-sop.inria.fr/morpheme/team.html>`_ to get access.
   
   **Read carefully IMPORTANT.TXT and README.TXT files in marsalt/morpheme**
   
   DO NOT MODIFY MORPHEME CODE DIRECTLY IN MARS-ALT REPOSITORY, DO IT IN morpheme-privat
